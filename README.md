# MVA Projects Portfolio (2024–2025)

This repository contains a collection of projects, assignments, practical sessions, and research work completed during the **Master Mathematics, Vision, Learning (MVA)** at **ENS Paris-Saclay** during the academic year **2024–2025**.

The goal of this repository is to centralize and showcase the work carried out throughout the program, covering topics in Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Deep Learning, Optimization, Probabilistic Modeling, Reinforcement Learning, and Multimodal AI.

---

## About the MVA Program

The MVA (Mathematics, Vision, Learning) Master is one of the leading graduate programs in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Computer Vision.

The curriculum combines strong theoretical foundations with hands-on projects and research-oriented assignments.
